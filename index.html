Brandon King

About

I am a PhD candidate and research assistant at the Centre for Human Factors and Sociotechnical systems. It is my goal to support the development and integration of brain-computer interface technologies into society as a force for good through the recommendation of prospective controls for the risks that they will introduce. Outside of my PhD, I engage in research projects in the fields of defence science, technology, and healthcare.
